Tigrinya Dictionary app

Tigrinya (ትግርኛ) is a language spoken in Eritrea and in northern Ethiopia in the Tigray region. It is also spoken by the global diaspora of these regions The aim of the Tigrinya dictionary app is to improve the language. This can be done mainly by adding more contents and updated the contents. The tigrinya dictionary app translate words between seven different languages. It translte words between Tigrinya, English, French, German, Italian, Spanish, Amharic and Arabic.

The work is still in progress and we will always need collabration from the Tigrinya speaking community about how we can improve the contents. So if there is anything that needs to be added, removed or updated please contact us on our social media links below or email us directly.
