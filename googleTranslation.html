Tigrinya in Google translate

The aim of this project is to add Tigrinya to list of languages in Google translate. Since the work has started google open us platform to contribute and validate. Now the community is working hard to increase the list of translated sentences from English to Tigrinya.

Steps to contribute in Google translate community

  • 1: Visit: https://translate.google.co.uk/

  • 2: Click on “community” icon (login required)

  • 3: Select “English-Tigrinya “ from drop down menu and click “Continue”

  • 4: Choose “Translate” or “Validate” to begin contribution.
